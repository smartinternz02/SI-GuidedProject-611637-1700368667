Safeguarding Agriculture : AI-Enables Prognostication of Farm Insect Threats

about

Agriculture, serving as the backbone of human sustenance, faces constant threats from harmful insects capable of causing substantial damage to crops, resulting in significant economic losses. Early identification and prediction of the presence of dangerous farm insects play a pivotal role in implementing effective pest control measures, ensuring the security of food production. In recent years, advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies have emerged as powerful tools for accurate and timely prediction of insect infestations on farms, revolutionizing the way farmers approach pest management.
